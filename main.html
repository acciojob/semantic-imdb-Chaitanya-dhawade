Summary

Interstellar (2014) follows Cooper, a former NASA pilot turned farmer, in a near-future Earth where crops are failing and humanity faces extinction. When a secret NASA program discovers a wormhole near Saturn, Cooper is recruited to lead a crew through it in search of a new habitable planet. Leaving behind his daughter Murph, he embarks on an extraordinary journey across space and time, confronting the limits of human endurance — and love — in the face of the unknown.
